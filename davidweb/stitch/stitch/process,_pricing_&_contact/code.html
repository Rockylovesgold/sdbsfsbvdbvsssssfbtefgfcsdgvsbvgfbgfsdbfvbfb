Common Questions

How long does a standard fix take? expand_more
Most standard fixes are completed within 24-48 hours. For more complex issues involving database corruption or deeper architectural problems, we'll provide a custom timeline in your proposal.
Do you offer guarantees on your work? expand_more
Absolutely. All our "Fix Pack" services come with a 30-day warranty. If the same bug reappears within that time, we fix it again for free, no questions asked.
What access do you need to my website? expand_more
Typically, we need an Administrator account for your WordPress dashboard and FTP/SFTP access to your hosting server. We use encrypted channels to share credentials securely.
Can I upgrade to a care plan later? expand_more
Yes! Many clients start with a one-off fix or performance boost to get their site in shape, and then switch to a monthly Care Plan to keep it that way.
